Assesment of the SdA

RUBRIC

🔎 Content: Research and solutions. 💬 Language: Grammar and vocabulary. 🎙️ Oral Fluency: Podcast quality. 🎨 Design: Infographic layout.

Rúbrica de ejemplo (4x4)
 EXCELLENTGOODSATISFACTORYNEEDS IMPROVEMENT
CONTENT&RESEARCHDeeply researched local issue with a very creative and realistic solution. (2.5)Clear local issue identified with a practical and logical solution. (1.75)The issue is mentioned but lacks local focus or the solution is vague. (1.50)No clear local issue or solution provided. Work is too general. (0.5)
LANGUAGEExcellent use of B2 vocabulary, connectors, and complex structures like conditionals. (2.5)Good use of vocabulary and connectors with very few errors. (1.75)Basic vocabulary used. Some errors in B2 structures (conditionals or connectors). (1.50)Limited vocabulary. Frequent grammar errors that hinder communication. (0.5)
ORAL FLUENCY(pódcast)Very natural flow, excellent intonation, and clear pronunciation. Sounds professional. (2.5)Clear and steady pace. Easy to follow and understand throughout. (1.75)Some hesitation or "reading" tone, but generally clear and audible. (1.50)Very hesitant, unclear pronunciation, or audio quality is too poor. (0.5)
DESIGN: infographicHighly professional layout. Perfect balance between text and visual elements. (2.5)Well-organized, attractive, and easy to read. Good use of icons and images. (1.75)Basic design. Some parts are slightly cluttered or hard to follow. (1.50)Unorganized, lacks visual appeal, or text is difficult to read. (0.5)

Podcast & Infographic: 70% of the final mark.
Extra Practice & Games: 30% of the final mark.

WELCOME: What are we going to do?

What are we going to do?

We are going to deal with the topic of the environment using English as the language of communication.

Throughout the four sessions you are going to do:

  • An individual podcast using Vocaroo.com/ mobile phone
  • A group infographic using digital tools such as Genially or Canva.

The main objective is to improve your speaking production and reflect about  the environmental problems affecting the Earth planet.

SESSION 2: Language and planning

Designing your infographic

    Goal: Visualizing Solutions

    In this session, you will work with your partner to design an infographic that summarizes the environmental challenge you analyzed in the previous session.

    Instructions:

    Step 1 - Planning: Review your notes. Select three causes and three solutions.
    Step 2 - Design: Use Canva or Genially.
    Step 3 - Content: Include a title, icons, and at least three new keywords (e.g., carbon footprint, sustainable...).
    Note: Be ready to present your draft at the end of the session!

    🛠️ Tools & Getting Started


    We recommend using one of these free online tools. Both have thousands of Infographic Templates ready for you:

    Canva: The most popular and easiest to use.Click here to see examples on Canva

    How to start: Search for "Infographic" in the search bar, pick a template you like, and double-click on the text to change it.


    Genially: Great if you want to add "interactive" buttons (pop-up text, videos).Click here to see interactive templates on Genially.

    How to start: Choose "Create Genially" > "Infographics".
    💡 Quick Step-by-Step:

    Sign up: Use your school email or Google account.
    Collaborate: If you work in pairs, one person creates the file and uses the "Share" button to invite the partner. Now you can both edit at the same time!
    Download: When finished, download your work as a PDF or PNG image.

SESSION 4: Reflection and peer feedback

Reflexction and peer feedback

Sharing our Vision

Now it is time to share your hard work with the rest of the class. We will have a "Gallery Walk" and a listening session.

The Process: You will present your infographic to your classmates and we will listen to some selected podcast clips.
Peer Feedback: Using a "Two Stars and a Wish" technique, you must provide feedback to at least two classmates. Write down two things you loved about their work (the stars) and one constructive suggestion for future projects (the wish).

Self-assesment

Think about your learning process during this unit:

Take a moment to reflect on your progress during this unit. Answer the following questions in your learning diary:

1. Which specific B2 vocabulary words or structures (like conditionals) do you feel more confident using now?
2. What was the most difficult part of researching a local environmental issue?
3. How has your digital competence improved by creating an infographic and a podcast?

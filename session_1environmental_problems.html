Understanding Environmental Perspectives

Duración:
30 minutos
Agrupamiento:
Pairs or small groups

1. Identifying key Information:

From video 1 (National Geografic): List three main causes of global warming mentioned by Bill Nye.
From video 2 ( BBC 6 Minute English): Summarize the two main solutions discussed in the conversation.


2. Vocabulary building

Choose five new expressions or words related to the environment that you found interesting ( e.g. carbon footprint, fossil fuels, sustainable). You must use at least three of them in your future infographic.

3. Critical Thinking

In pairs, discuss and answer the following question:

" Which environmental challenge mentioned in the videos do you think is the most urgent in our city/town/ region? Why?
